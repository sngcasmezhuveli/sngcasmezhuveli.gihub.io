Sree Narayana Guru College Of Advanced Studies, (SNGCAS) is a Centre of Excellence in ‘higher learning’ which is unique and different not only for the guiding philosophy and values, but also for the innovative learning practices. SNGIST is led by the Sree Narayana Trust,Kollam from the noble intentions and farsighted vision of a few leader figureheads in their own communities and with years of experience in business and government.The mission of SNGCAS is to enable students to explore the resources available to them and make their experience of it unique, personal and internalized for growth and to undergo a transformational learning. It is represented through the core values – Competence: The sum of knowledge and skills, Confidence: To think critically about performance and compare oneself and Compassion: Empathy and social conscience.

The institution offers graduate courses in B.com computer application & Bca degree course under Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam and approved by AICTE and is ISO 9001-2008 certified. Apart from this, the college offers add on programs taking into account today’s context. The campus is situated on 12 acres of land. It is a Wi-Fi campus with 400 computers and modern computer labs housing state-of-the-art technology. The mail server of SNGCAS is now hosted by Google. The eco-friendly campus provides opportunities to students to engage in various sports like basketball, volleyball and shuttle badminton.

The Library at SNGCAS is an outstanding learning resource center catering to information requirements of students. The library has more than 20000 volumes of text books and over 130 journals/magazines and access to over 1600 e-journals. In addition, e-learning facilities are also there. Hostel for boys and girls with excellent amenities have been provided inside the college campus.
